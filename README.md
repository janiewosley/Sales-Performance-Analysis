# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer metrics, and brand-wise sales trends. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), compare business performance across different dimensions, and make data-driven decisions.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance using key sales KPIs.
- Analyze sales revenue, profit, and sales cost.
- Compare sales profit across different brands.
- Evaluate monthly sales trends.
- Analyze customer count and purchasing behavior.
- Identify profitability by income level.
- Compare sales cost across product colors.
- Support business decision-making through interactive visualizations.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Regional Analysis
- Interactive Region slicer
- Compare sales performance across:
  - North
  - South
  - East
  - West

### Category Filter
Analyze performance by product category:
- Accessories
- Electronics

### Brand Performance
Visualizes **Sales Profit by Brand**, including:
- Apple
- Lenovo
- Samsung
- HP
- Asus
- Dell

### Monthly Sales Analysis
Waterfall chart showing:
- Monthly Sales Amount
- Customer Growth
- Overall Sales Trend

### Income Level Analysis
Compare sales profit based on:
- Medium Income
- High Income
- Low Income

### Product Cost Analysis
Sales Cost comparison by color:
- White
- Gray
- Black

### Yearly Revenue Analysis
Displays annual Sales Revenue performance.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Apple generated the highest sales profit among all brands.
- Total Sales Revenue reached **3 Million**.
- Total Profit exceeded **932 Thousand**.
- Medium-income customers contributed the highest sales profit.
- White-colored products recorded the highest sales cost.
- Monthly sales show consistent growth throughout the reporting period.
- The dashboard provides an executive-level overview of business performance.
